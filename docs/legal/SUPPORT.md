## Getting started

### First launch setup

On first open, BuxMuse shows a **5-card onboarding wizard**. You can **Skip** at any time before the last card. Nothing requires a BuxMuse account. You can replay it anytime from **Settings → Replay onboarding guide**.

**01 — Confirm region & currency**  
On the **Regional & budget** card, review country/region and display currency (BuxMuse detects your device automatically). Change them if needed.

**02 — Set your budget baseline (optional)**  
Turn on **consumer debt** tracking if you want it. Set an **optional spending cap**, or leave it at zero to use your full logged income.

**03 — Learn about Studio (optional)**  
The **Studio suite** card explains Simple and Pro Studio. No purchase required during setup — activate later in **Settings → Studio**.

**04 — Turn on backup & security (optional)**  
On **Backup & protection**, optionally enable Face ID / Touch ID, a passcode, and local backup reminders. Your data stays on this device until you export a backup or turn on iCloud sync.

**05 — Get started & take the tour**  
Tap **Get Started**. An interactive app tour may start automatically. Then start your **7-day free trial** to unlock the app and log your first income from Home.

**Later (not required at first launch)**

- **iCloud sync** — **Settings → iCloud sync** when you want a second device
- **Categories** — choose when adding income or expenses
- **Wallet import** — optional in Expenses when you need it

### Install & explore

1. **Install** BuxMuse from the App Store or TestFlight.
2. Complete the **onboarding wizard** above.
3. Explore **Home**, **Expenses**, **Goals**, and **Settings** after the optional app tour.

### Your 7-day free trial

- BuxMuse and **Pro Studio** offer a **7-day free trial** through Apple’s subscription flow (monthly and yearly plans).
- **Simple Studio** is a one-time purchase with **no trial**.
- After onboarding, tap **Start 7-day free trial** on the paywall (or in **Settings → Subscription**) to begin. Apple manages billing and renewal.
- Cancel anytime in **Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions** before the trial ends to avoid being charged.
- If you already used a trial in the same subscription group, Apple may show the regular price instead.

### Core features (no extra purchase)

- **Expenses & income** — manual entry, categories, search, and insights.
- **Budgets & goals** — track spending against your plan.
- **Backup** — export an encrypted `.buxmuse` file via Settings and save it to Files, iCloud Drive, or email.

### Optional features

| Feature | Where | Notes |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Studio (Simple or Pro)** | Settings → Studio | Sold separately; requires an active BuxMuse subscription |
| **Personal iCloud sync** | Settings → iCloud sync | Optional; uses your private iCloud via Apple CloudKit |
| **Apple Wallet import** | Expenses (when enabled) | You pick which transactions to import |
| **HealthKit sleep** | Pro Studio → Creative Energy | Optional; read-only on your device |
| **Face ID lock** | Settings → Security | Optional app lock |

### Studio add-ons (optional)

- **BuxMuse subscription** — required for app access. Start with a 7-day free trial or subscribe directly.
- **Simple Studio** — one-time add-on: invoices, work ledger, up to 3 workspaces.
- **Pro Studio** — subscription add-on: tax tools, PDF designer, unlimited projects (includes Simple).

Studio add-ons do **not** replace BuxMuse. Manage everything under **Settings → Subscription**.

## Frequently asked questions

### Account & data

**Do I need a BuxMuse account?**  
No. There is no BuxMuse sign-up. Your data stays on your iPhone or iPad unless you turn on iCloud sync or export a backup.

**Where is my data stored?**  
On your device. Optional **Personal iCloud sync** stores encrypted records in **your** iCloud account (Apple CloudKit), not on BuxMuse servers.

**How do I back up my data?**  
Go to **Settings → Backup** and create a `.buxmuse` file. You choose where to save it (Files, iCloud Drive, AirDrop, email).

**How do I move to a new iPhone?**  
Use **iCloud sync** (if enabled on both devices) or restore from a `.buxmuse` backup on the new device.

**How do I delete everything?**  
**Settings → Data** — delete all local data. If iCloud sync is on, you can choose to keep or delete your iCloud copy.

### Subscriptions

**What do I need to buy?**  
An active **BuxMuse** subscription (monthly or yearly) is required to use the app after onboarding unless you are in an active free trial. **Studio** is optional.

**How do I cancel?**  
**Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions** on your iPhone. Cancelling stops future charges; access continues until the period ends.

**How do I restore purchases?**  
**Settings → Subscription → Restore purchases**. Use the same Apple ID you used to buy.

**I bought Studio but the app says I need BuxMuse.**  
Studio add-ons require an active BuxMuse subscription. They unlock Studio features; they do not replace the base app subscription.

**Can I get a refund?**  
Purchases are handled by Apple. Request a refund at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).

**Why is the price in dollars / another currency?**  
Prices follow your **App Store country** and Apple’s local pricing, not the currency you use for budgets inside the app.

### Studio

**What is Simple Studio?**  
Invoices, a work ledger, scanning, and up to **3** active workspaces. One-time purchase on top of BuxMuse.

**What is Pro Studio?**  
Tax Studio, PDF invoice design, unlimited projects, and Pro tools. Subscription on top of BuxMuse; includes Simple.

**How do I enable Studio?**  
Purchase an add-on under **Settings → Subscription**, then turn Studio on in **Settings → Studio**.

### Privacy & permissions

**Does BuxMuse sell my data?**  
No. See our [Privacy Policy](https://buxmuse.com/privacy-policy).

**Why does BuxMuse ask for Camera, Photos, Health, or Location?**  
Only when you use those features (receipt scan, optional sleep sync, optional mileage location). You can deny permission and still use the rest of the app.

## Troubleshooting

### Subscriptions & purchases

| Problem | What to try |
|--------|-------------|
| **“App Store products could not be loaded”** | Check internet connection. Wait a few hours after a new app version is submitted. Ensure you are on the latest TestFlight or App Store build. Sign in with a **Sandbox** account only when testing TestFlight (Settings → Developer → Sandbox Apple Account). |
| **“This product is not available right now”** | Same as above; often means the product is not yet active in App Store Connect for your build. Update the app and try again later. |
| **Purchase succeeded but access did not unlock** | Tap **Restore purchases** in **Settings → Subscription**. Force-quit and reopen the app. |
| **Wrong Apple ID charged** | Purchases use your App Store Apple ID. Sign in with the correct account under **Settings → [your name] → Media & Purchases**. |

### iCloud sync

| Problem | What to try |
|--------|-------------|
| **Sync will not turn on** | Confirm you are signed into iCloud on the device (**Settings → [your name] → iCloud**). Enable iCloud Drive. Check network connection. |
| **Data not appearing on second device** | Enable sync on both devices with the **same Apple ID**. Wait a few minutes. Open the app on the second device while online. |
| **Sync conflict message** | Use the in-app **Sync Conflict** screen to choose which version to keep. |

### Backups & restore

| Problem | What to try |
|--------|-------------|
| **Cannot find `.buxmuse` file** | Check Files or iCloud Drive where you saved the export. |
| **Restore failed** | Ensure the file is a valid BuxMuse backup. Try exporting a fresh backup from the source device. |

### Receipt scan & Wallet import

| Problem | What to try |
|--------|-------------|
| **Camera or Photos denied** | **Settings → BuxMuse → Camera / Photos** → Allow. |
| **Wallet import missing** | Enable Wallet sync in BuxMuse Settings if available. Requires a supported device and iOS version. You choose transactions each time — there is no background bank sync. |
| **Scanned amount wrong** | Edit the expense before saving. OCR is a helper, not a guarantee. |

### HealthKit (Pro)

| Problem | What to try |
|--------|-------------|
| **Sleep data not showing** | Enable Health access in **Settings → Health → Data Access → BuxMuse**. Ensure sleep data exists in Apple Health. |
| **Health prompt never appeared** | Open **Pro Studio → Creative Energy** and follow the in-app consent flow. |

### App lock

| Problem | What to try |
|--------|-------------|
| **Face ID / Touch ID not working** | Enroll Face ID or Touch ID in **Settings → Face ID & Passcode**. Enable app lock in BuxMuse **Settings → Security**. |

### Performance & display

| Problem | What to try |
|--------|-------------|
| **App feels slow after large import** | Normal after importing many expenses or enabling sync on a large library. Leave the app open on Wi‑Fi briefly. |
| **Wrong language or currency** | **Settings → Region & language** (or onboarding region). Budget currency is separate from App Store subscription currency. |

### Still stuck?

Email **support@buxmuse.com** with:

- iPhone or iPad model and iOS version  
- BuxMuse version (Settings → About)  
- What you expected vs what happened  
- Screenshots if helpful  

Please do **not** attach financial backups unless we ask — they contain personal data.
