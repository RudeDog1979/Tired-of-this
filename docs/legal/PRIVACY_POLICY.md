# BuxMuse Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 6 July 2026  
**Contact:** support@buxmuse.com  
**App:** BuxMuse (`com.buxmuse.app`)  
**Publisher:** Rodolfo Zorrilla Pena

---

## Summary

BuxMuse is an **offline-first** personal finance and studio companion for iPhone and iPad. We do **not** require a BuxMuse account. We do **not** operate a BuxMuse cloud that stores your expenses, receipts, or budgets.

Your financial information stays on your device unless **you** choose optional Apple iCloud sync, household sharing with people you invite, or export an encrypted backup file yourself.

---

## Data stored on your device

The app stores information you enter or import locally, including:

- Expenses, income, budgets, goals, debts, and settings  
- Optional Studio data (invoices, projects, agreements, receipts, mileage, and related files)  
- Optional receipt photos you capture or select  
- Optional household shared expenses (when you use household sync)  
- Encrypted backup files (`.buxmuse`) you create and choose where to save (iCloud Drive, email, Files, etc.)

We do not receive this data unless **you** send a backup file or message to us by email or other means you control.

You can delete local data by removing the app or using in-app reset and backup tools.

---

## Subscriptions and in-app purchases

BuxMuse offers **auto-renewable subscriptions** and **optional in-app purchases** (Studio add-ons) through the **Apple App Store**.

- **Billing:** Apple processes all payments. BuxMuse does not receive or store your payment card details.  
- **Products:** BuxMuse offers two auto-renewable subscription tiers. **Standard** unlocks the personal-finance app and Simple Studio. **Pro** includes everything in Standard plus Pro Studio tools. Access after any free trial is through an active subscription purchased via Apple.  
- **Trials and pricing:** Free trials, prices, and renewal terms are shown in the app before you purchase and may vary by country.  
- **Manage or cancel:** Open **Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions** on your iPhone or iPad, or use **Manage Subscriptions** in the App Store.  
- **Refunds:** Refund requests are handled by Apple under Apple’s policies.

Subscription status is verified on your device through Apple’s StoreKit. BuxMuse does not operate a separate billing account for you.

**Terms of Use (EULA):** [Apple Standard EULA](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/dev/stdeula/)

## iCloud sync and household sharing (optional)

If you turn on **Sync with iCloud** or **Household sharing**, BuxMuse uses **Apple iCloud and CloudKit** to store encrypted sync records in **your** (or your household’s) private iCloud account.

- BuxMuse **does not** operate a server that receives your synced expenses, budgets, or Studio data.  
- BuxMuse **cannot** read, access, or view your iCloud data.  
- **Personal sync** is limited to devices signed in with the same Apple ID.  
- **Household sharing** is limited to people you explicitly invite through Apple’s sharing flow.

You can turn iCloud sync off at any time in Settings. Deleting local data while sync is on may affect copies stored in iCloud, depending on the options you choose in the app.

---

## Camera, photos, and location

- **Camera / Photo Library:** Used when you choose to scan or attach receipt images, import documents, or add photos in Studio. Images stay on your device unless you export a backup or share them yourself.  
- **Location (when in use):** Used only for optional Studio mileage address capture when you use that feature. Location is not collected in the background for tracking.

---

## Face ID and device passcode

Optional app lock uses **Face ID**, **Touch ID**, or your device passcode through iOS. Credentials are handled by the system Keychain on your device, not on BuxMuse servers.

---

## Notifications and Live Activities

BuxMuse uses **local** notifications (for example subscription reminders and in-app alerts you enable). We do not use remote push notifications to track you.

**Live Activities** (for example Studio timers on the Lock Screen) display information you start in the app and are managed by iOS on your device.

---

## Limited network use

BuxMuse works without a BuxMuse account and does **not** upload your expenses, receipts, budgets, or Studio records to BuxMuse servers.

The app may connect to the internet for **non-personal** or **minimal** purposes:

| Purpose | What is sent | Your personal finance data? |
|--------|----------------|-----------------------------|
| **Tax rate catalog** | Download of public reference tax JSON | No |
| **In-app tips / news** | Download of public announcement JSON | No |
| **Merchant icons** | A website domain name (for example `amazon.com`) to fetch a favicon from public icon services | No expense amounts or account details |

These requests do not include your name, email, or BuxMuse account (there is none). Merchant icon lookups use a domain derived from a merchant name to display an icon; no full transaction history is transmitted.

If you email **support@buxmuse.com**, we receive whatever you choose to include in that message.

---

## Analytics, advertising, and tracking

BuxMuse does **not** use third-party advertising SDKs. BuxMuse does **not** use third-party analytics SDKs to track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

Optional **diagnostic export** in Settings creates a report on your device that **you** choose to share. BuxMuse does not automatically upload diagnostics.

## Children

BuxMuse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your rights and contact

Because your data stays on your device by default, you control it through the app, iOS, and optional iCloud settings. You may:

- Delete the app to remove local data stored in the app sandbox  
- Use in-app backup, restore, and reset tools  
- Turn off iCloud sync at any time  
- Contact us with privacy questions at **support@buxmuse.com**

If you are in the UK, EEA, or other regions with privacy laws, you may contact us at the email above to ask about your data. Because BuxMuse does not operate a user account system or central database of your finances, we generally cannot access your on-device or iCloud-stored data unless you send it to us.
