# BuxMuse Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 3 June 2026  
**Contact:** [your support email]  
**App:** BuxMuse (`com.buxmuse.app`)

## Summary

BuxMuse is an **offline-first** personal finance and studio companion. We do **not** require an account. We do **not** operate a BuxMuse cloud that stores your expenses, receipts, or health data.

## Data stored on your device

The app stores information you enter or import locally, including:

- Expenses, income, budgets, goals, and settings  
- Optional Studio data (invoices, projects, receipts, mileage)  
- Optional receipt photos you capture or select  
- Encrypted backup files (`.buxmuse`) you create and choose where to save (iCloud Drive, email, Files, etc.)

We do not receive this data unless **you** send a backup file to us by email or other means you control.

## Apple Health (optional)

If you enable **HealthKit sleep sync** (Pro Creative Energy), BuxMuse reads **sleep analysis** from Apple Health **on your iPhone only** to improve your Creative Energy score. You can turn this off anytime in Settings or iOS Health → Data Access.

- BuxMuse does **not** sell or share Health data.  
- BuxMuse does **not** upload Health data to BuxMuse servers.  
- BuxMuse does not write health data to Apple Health today.

## Camera, photos, and location

- **Camera / Photo Library:** Used to scan or attach receipt images when you choose.  
- **Location (when in use):** Used only for optional Studio mileage address capture when you use that feature.

## Face ID / device passcode

Optional app lock uses **Face ID** or device passcode via iOS; your passcode is stored in the device Keychain, not on our servers.

## Notifications

BuxMuse uses **local** notifications (e.g. subscription reminders). We do not use remote push notifications to track you.

## Analytics and tracking

BuxMuse does **not** use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs. We do **not** track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

## Children

BuxMuse is not directed at children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date will change. Continued use of the app after updates means you accept the revised policy.

## Your rights

Because data stays on your device, you can delete the app to remove local data, or use in-app reset/backup tools. For questions: [your support email].
